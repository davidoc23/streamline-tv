# Privacy Policy for StreamlineTV

**Effective Date:** 23-04-2026

StreamlineTV (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the StreamlineTV mobile application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

* Email address
* Authentication data (via Firebase Authentication)

---

### b. Usage Data

We may collect basic usage information such as:

* App interactions (e.g. subscribed shows, watched episodes)
* Device information (e.g. device type, operating system)

---

### c. Locally Stored Data

StreamlineTV stores the following data **on your device**:

* Subscribed TV shows
* Episode watch progress

This data is stored locally and is not shared with our servers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

* Authenticate and manage your account
* Provide core app functionality (subscriptions and tracking)
* Improve app performance and user experience

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

StreamlineTV uses the following third-party services:

### Firebase (Google)

Used for:

* User authentication
* Session management

Firebase may collect and process data in accordance with its own Privacy Policy.

---

### TVMaze API

Used to:

* Fetch TV show data (titles, episodes, summaries)

No personal user data is sent to TVMaze.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

* Account authentication is handled securely via Firebase.
* Subscription and watch progress data are stored locally on your device.
* We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

---

## 6. Data Retention

* Your account data is retained as long as your account exists.
* Local data remains on your device until the app is uninstalled or data is cleared.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

* Access your data
* Delete your account (via Firebase)
* Stop using the app at any time

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

StreamlineTV is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new “Effective Date”.
